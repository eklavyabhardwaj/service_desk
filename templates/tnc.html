Terms and Conditions for Raising a Service Ticket

1. Eligibility for Service Ticket Submission

  • Service tickets can only be raised by authorized personnel or registered customers.
  • The machine must be under a valid warranty or covered under an active service contract.
  • For out-of-warranty machines, service charges will apply as per company policy.
  • The company reserves the right to refuse service for unauthorized or tampered machines.

2. Information Required for Service Request

  • The user must provide the machine's serial number, model, and purchase details.
  • A clear and detailed description of the issue, including relevant error codes or images, must be submitted.
  • Accurate contact details of the requester must be provided for effective communication.

3. Service Ticket Processing

  • Once a ticket is raised, a unique service reference number will be assigned for tracking purposes.
  • The service team will review the request and determine whether remote assistance or an on-site visit is required.
  • Response time may vary based on issue severity, location, and availability of service personnel.
  • The company reserves the right to prioritize service requests based on urgency.

4. Service Charges and Warranty Coverage

  • If the issue falls under warranty, repair or replacement will be provided as per warranty terms.
  • If the issue is due to user negligence, physical damage, or external factors, additional charges will apply.
  • A repair cost estimate will be communicated before proceeding with chargeable repairs.
  • Customers must confirm approval for chargeable repairs before service execution.

5. Customer Responsibilities

  • The customer must ensure that the machine is accessible for inspection and service.
  • A backup of important data should be taken before requesting service, as the company is not liable for data loss.
  • Any unauthorized modifications or repairs by third parties will void the warranty or service contract.
  • The customer must provide a safe and suitable working environment for the service personnel.

6. Cancellation and Rescheduling

  • Service appointments must be canceled or rescheduled at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Failure to do so may result in a rescheduling fee or forfeiture of any pre-paid service fees.
  • The company reserves the right to reschedule or cancel service appointments due to unforeseen circumstances.

7. Limitation of Liability

  • The company is not responsible for consequential damages arising due to machine failure.
  • Liability is strictly limited to the repair or replacement of defective parts as per service terms.
  • The company is not liable for delays in service due to factors beyond its control.

8. Dispute Resolution

  • Any disputes regarding service must be reported within 7 days of service completion.
  • The company reserves the right to investigate and resolve disputes at its discretion.
  • Legal disputes, if any, shall be settled under the jurisdiction of the company's registered location.

By raising a service ticket, the customer agrees to abide by these Terms and Conditions.
